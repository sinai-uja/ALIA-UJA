### Description
*Brief summary of dataset content and origin.*
Set of clinical guidelines and medical practice documents produced by the Andalusian Health Service (SAS). Includes diagnostic and therapeutic recommendations for healthcare professionals. Taken from the ‘Guidelines and advice’ section of the official SAS publications portal.

### Purpose
*Reason and objectives for creating the dataset.*
The dataset was created with the aim of centralising, structuring and facilitating access to clinical guidelines published by the Andalusian Health Service, enabling their analysis, search and reuse in contexts supporting medical practice, health training or the development of clinical information systems.
