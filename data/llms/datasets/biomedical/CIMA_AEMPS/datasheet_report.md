### Dataset Name
Centro de Información online de Medicamentos Autorizados (CIMA) de la Agencia Española de Medicamentos y Productos Sanitarios (AEMPS)

### Description
*Brief summary of dataset content and origin.*
Dataset consisting of more than 15,000 medicines (authorized, revoked and temporarily suspended), and around 1,600 active ingredients. For each drug, it includes the official reference documents for the correct use of the drugs, such as technical data sheets, package inserts and public evaluation reports.
