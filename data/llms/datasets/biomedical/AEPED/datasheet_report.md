## General Information
### Dataset ID
AEPED

### Dataset Name
AEPED: Pediamécum de la Asociación Española de Pediatría: Fichas de medicamentos y principios activos de uso pediátrico

### Description
*Brief summary of dataset content and origin.*
Dataset from the Pediamécum of the Spanish Paediatrics Association (AEPED) that includes files on more than 660 medicines and active ingredients for paediatric use, with basic clinical and pharmacological information.
