### Dataset Name
Reports, publications, events, and agreements on Transfusion Medicine from the Ministry of Health

### Description
*Brief summary of dataset content and origin.*
The Ministry of Health's website dedicated to Transfusion Medicine offers a compendium of key resources and documents to ensure the safety and efficacy of blood donation and transfusion in Spain. It includes information on the structure of the National Transfusion Safety System, agreements and recommendations issued by bodies such as the National Hemotherapy Commission, and relevant legislative aspects. It also addresses haemovigilance issues, promotes voluntary and altruistic donation, and provides access to reports, publications, conferences, and events related to the field of transfusion. This portal is an essential tool for healthcare professionals and entities involved in the management and supervision of transfusion medicine in the country.
