### Description
*Brief summary of dataset content and origin.*
CARMEN-I is a clinical corpus in Spanish containing anonymised medical documents from Hospital Clínic de Barcelona, collected between March 2020 and March 2022. Its main objective is to provide a realistic dataset for the development and evaluation of natural language processing (NLP) tools applied to the clinical field in Spanish, while preserving patient privacy.

### Instances Description
*What each dataset instance contains.*
1,201 imaging reports; 617 discharge reports; 172 referral letters; 5 death reports; and 5 progress notes. Discharge reports and referral letters are not presented in full. Instead, they were divided into sections as stated above due to their excessive length, resulting in: 189 medical histories; 154 progress notes; 72 physical exams; 61 medical tests; 25 surgery reports; 31 treatment plans; 176 current problems; 40 imaging; and 41 follow-up sections.
