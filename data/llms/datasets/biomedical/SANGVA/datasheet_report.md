### Description
*Brief summary of dataset content and origin.*
Set of clinical practice guidelines, care protocols, clinical pathways, and clinical action documents developed by the Regional Ministry of Health of the Valencian Government. The resource includes evidence-based recommendations for different medical specialties and levels of care.

### Purpose
*Reason and objectives for creating the dataset.*
The dataset was created with the aim of centralising and structuring the clinical guidelines and official healthcare documents of the Valencian public health system, facilitating their consultation, analysis and reuse in clinical and academic contexts or in the development of digital tools to support medical decision-making.
