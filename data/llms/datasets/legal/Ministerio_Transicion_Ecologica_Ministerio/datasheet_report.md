### Description
*Brief summary of dataset content and origin.*
The “Ministry” section of the website of the Ministry for Ecological Transition and Demographic Challenge (MITECO) provides a comprehensive overview of the ministry's structure, functions, and activities. It includes detailed information on its organization, highlighting the various secretariats and commissioners, such as the Secretary of State for Energy and the Secretary of State for the Environment. It also presents the public bodies attached to it, such as the State Meteorological Agency (AEMET) and the Hydrographic Confederations. The section provides access to strategic documents and plans, such as the Integrated National Energy and Climate Plan, the Just Transition Strategy, and the Green Public Procurement Plan. In addition, specific initiatives related to the Mar Menor and Doñana are detailed, as well as information on public employment, training, scholarships, and internships. Resources for citizens are also provided, including service units, public participation, and services by area of activity. Overall, this section acts as a documentation and consultation center for understanding MITECO's policies and actions in key areas such as energy, the environment, and demographic challenges.
