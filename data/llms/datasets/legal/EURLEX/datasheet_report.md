### Dataset Name
Documentos pertenecientes al EUR-Lex

### Description
*Brief summary of dataset content and origin.*
EUR-Lex documents include EU legislation (treaties, regulations, directives, decisions), case law from the Court of Justice of the EU, legislative proposals, preparatory documents, and editions of the Official Journal. They are available in all official EU languages, with CELEX identifiers and free access in HTML and PDF formats.

### Purpose
*Reason and objectives for creating the dataset.*
The EUR-Lex dataset was created to ensure public and transparent access to European Union law, facilitate its uniform application in Member States, and support research, legal interpretation, and decision-making at European level. It also promotes the official, multilingual dissemination of EU legal acts and institutional documents.
