### Description
*Brief summary of dataset content and origin.*
The Studies and Publications page of the Ministry of Housing and Urban Agenda of Spain offers a compilation of technical and strategic documents related to housing, urban planning and sustainability. Among the available materials are reports from the Housing Observatory, catalogs of good practices in architecture and urban planning, accessibility guides in urbanized public spaces, and studies such as the "White Paper on Sustainability in Spanish Urban Planning". These documents provide detailed analyses, recommendations and regulatory frameworks that support public policies on housing and sustainable urban development.
