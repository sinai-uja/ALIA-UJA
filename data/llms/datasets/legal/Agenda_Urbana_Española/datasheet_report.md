These documents aim to reflect the sharing and dissemination of knowledge, experiences and good practices that have an impact on towns and cities through the Spanish Urban Agenda, with examples that, due to their characteristics and specifications, can contribute to the fulfillment of some of the objectives of the strategic framework while stimulating creativity and providing viable solutions for sustainable urban development.
